Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Welcome to Ask Lawyer (asklawyer.in). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our AI Legal Assistant platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Chat Data: The legal queries and text you type into the chat box to generate responses. (Note: Please avoid sharing highly sensitive personal information like Aadhar numbers, bank details, or real names in the chat).
  • Session Information: We use an anonymous, randomly generated `session_id` (stored in your browser's Session Storage) to remember the context of your ongoing chat. This memory is temporary and resets when you start a new session.
  • Log Data: Standard information your browser sends automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and time of visit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly to:

  • Provide you with accurate AI-generated legal guidance and document drafting.
  • Maintain the conversational memory (context) during your active chat session.
  • Analyze website performance and improve user experience.

3. Cookies and Google AdSense

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Additionally, we use third-party advertising companies like Google AdSense to serve ads when you visit our website.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie for personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. The chat processing is done via secure API connections (e.g., Groq Llama3). However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
